## üîÑ How It Works

1. **Generate Content**: Click the extension icon to see AI-generated post ideas based on your profile and preferences
2. **Customize & Schedule**: Edit the content to match your voice and schedule for optimal posting times
3. **Engage Strategically**: Receive suggestions for meaningful comments on targeted posts in your network
4. **Track Performance**: Monitor how your content performs and get AI-powered suggestions for improvement
5. **Grow Your Network**: Build connections through consistent, valuable engagement
